# Competitors

How we stack up, and the line to use when a lead is shopping around.

## Big-box vinyl (the cheap option)
- **Who:** home-improvement store house brands, online vinyl blind sites.
- **Strength:** price, instant availability.
- **Weakness:** DIY measure/install (high error rate), thin slats, no service,
  no motorization help, no warranty support.
- **Our line:** "We measure, we install, we stand behind it. A $40 blind that's
  cut wrong is a $40 mistake you live with for ten years." (We lost [[Helen Park]]
  here — worth a budget-tier blinds offering to compete.)

## Online-only custom (mail-order)
- **Who:** national made-to-order shade sites.
- **Strength:** big fabric selection, decent pricing.
- **Weakness:** customer measures themselves; mistakes are on them; weeks of
  back-and-forth; no local install.
- **Our line:** "Same custom quality, but a real person measures so it fits the
  first time — and we hang it."

## Local full-service rival ("Premier Window Co")
- **Who:** established local installer, overlaps our market.
- **Strength:** reputation, showroom.
- **Weakness:** slower quotes, pushes high-end only, less responsive on small jobs.
- **Our line:** speed and responsiveness — same-week measure, texts answered fast.
