# Operations - Install

**When:** every confirmed install, after product arrives and is QC-checked.

**Steps:**
1. Confirm appointment + access notes from the customer note (gates, pets, parking).
2. Re-measure on site before drilling — trust nothing, verify mount type.
3. Inside mount: check depth clearance. Outside mount: confirm overlap for light gaps.
4. Install, test operation (cordless/motorized cycle), level check.
5. Walk the customer through operation + care; leave warranty card.
6. Photograph finished openings; attach to the customer note timeline.

**Gotchas / lessons learned (dated):**
- 2026-06-20 — blackout shades leak light at the edges on inside mounts; quote
  side channels or outside mount when true darkening is the goal.
